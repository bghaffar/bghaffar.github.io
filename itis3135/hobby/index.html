Who We Are

Geography Games is a fun and educational platform aimed at helping users learn about world geography in an engaging way.

Hi, my name is Bita! and I am a Computer Science student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNCC). I realized that whenever I took a break from studying, I wanted to relax with a fun game that was actually beneficial. Instead of wasting my time scrolling on social media, I figured why not combine relaxation with learning? That is when I decided to create Geography Games, a platform where I could make the best use of my downtime and at the same time help others do the same. Geography Games is a fun and educational platform aimed at helping users learn about world geography in an engaging way. Our team consists of passionate educators and game developers who believe in making learning enjoyable. Whether you’re a geography enthusiast or just starting to learn, Geography Games offers a way to explore the world and have fun at the same time!
