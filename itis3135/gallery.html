Bench Press – Chest & Triceps
Keep your back neutral and wrists aligned.
Back Squat – Quads & Glutes
Knees over toes, engage your core.
Bicep Curl – Arms
Don’t swing; isolate the biceps.
Crunches – Core
Lift with abs, not your neck.
Lunges – Quads, Glutes & Hamstrings
Keep your front knee over the ankle, back upright.
Deadlift – Glutes & Hamstrings
Hinge at hips; don’t round your back.
Lat Pull – Back
Pull elbows down, avoid leaning back.
Overhead Press – Shoulders
Brace your core and press straight up.
Plank – Core Stability
Keep spine straight and glutes tight.
Pull-Up – Back & Biceps
Use full range of motion.
Push-Up – Chest & Triceps
Keep elbows tucked and core engaged.
Stretching – Muscle Recovery
Hold each stretch for 15–30 seconds.
