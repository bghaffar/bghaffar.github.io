Website Evaluations

Fossil

The Fossil website demonstrates strong design principles, creating a clean and user-friendly experience.

  • Contrast: The color palette, featuring brown and black, complements the brand and enhances readability.
  • Repetition: Font style and spacing remain consistent throughout the site, creating a cohesive experience.
  • Alignment: Visuals are perfectly aligned with product descriptions and images, offering a professional and organized layout.
  • Proximity: Key information, like prices, is clearly separated and bolded, making it easy for users to find important details quickly.

Jasmine Grill & Kabob

This restaurant website struggles with readability and consistency across its pages.

  • Contrast: The font color and size make the menu items hard to read, especially against the background.
  • Repetition: There is inconsistency in the capitalization of headings. For example, the main heading displays "Jasmine Grill & kabob" with improper capitalization.
  • Alignment: The placement of the Instagram, Facebook, and Google Maps icons in the left corner feels disconnected from the rest of the layout.
  • Proximity: The small social media icons do not visually relate to other elements and feel isolated from the rest of the design.
